What is Scalping?

Scalping is a sophisticated trading strategy that capitalizes on small price movements in highly liquid markets, with the objective of capturing rapid, high-volume profits through multiple intraday trades. Unlike traditional trading approaches that target substantial price movements over extended periods, scalping focuses on extracting minimal price differentials through precision timing and execution. RustRocket's advanced Scalping technology utilizes microsecond analytics to identify optimal entry and exit points within volatile Crypto markets, enabling traders to accumulate significant profits through high-frequency, low-risk transactions.

Mathematical Foundation

The core mathematics of scalping is rooted in the profit formula, which must be optimized through high-frequency execution:

Profit = (exit_price - entry_price) * volume - fees
For profitable scalping: (exit_price - entry_price) > fees/volume
