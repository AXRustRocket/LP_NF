What is Sandwich Trading?

Sandwich Trading is a sophisticated MEV (Miner Extractable Value) strategy that involves identifying a pending trade in the mempool, then executing two strategic trades: one immediately before and one immediately after the target transaction. This "sandwiches" the original trade between your transactions, allowing you to profit from the price impact of the targeted transaction.
