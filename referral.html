Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a referral last?

Referrals are credited to your account for as long as the referred user maintains an active subscription. If they cancel and later rejoin, you'll still be credited as their referrer.

How are commissions paid out?

Commissions are paid as trading credits that are automatically applied to your account. These credits can be used to pay for your subscription or trading fees.

When do my tier benefits update?

Your tier is updated in real-time as soon as you reach the required number of active referrals. Any exclusive benefits will be activated immediately.

Can I track my referrals?

Yes, you can track all your referrals, their status, and your earned commissions through your dashboard in the "Referrals" section.
