What is Arbitrage?

Arbitrage is the strategic practice of simultaneously purchasing and selling identical or similar assets across different markets to capitalize on price discrepancies, generating risk-free profits. In the Crypto space, these inefficiencies are especially prevalent due to varying liquidity levels and market sentiment across exchanges. RustRocket's advanced arbitrage system leverages a global network of dedicated nodes strategically positioned in key financial centers, enabling near-instantaneous detection and exploitation of these price differentials before they normalize. Mathematical Foundation

The arbitrage profit formula is deceptively simple, but its successful execution is highly complex, requiring perfect synchronization:

Profit = (higher_price - lower_price) * volume - transaction_costs
Profitable when: (higher_price - lower_price) > transaction_costs/volume
